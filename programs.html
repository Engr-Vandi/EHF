Thematic Programs

Our Core Programs

Nine integrated thematic areas delivering humanitarian relief and long-term sustainable development across Nigeria.

What We Do

9 Thematic Areas of Impact

EHF implements holistic programs that address urgent humanitarian needs while building community resilience for the long term.

Program 01

Child Protection

Safeguarding children from abuse, neglect, exploitation, and violence while promoting child rights awareness and protective community systems.

  • Child protection awareness campaigns
  • Support for vulnerable and orphaned children
  • Prevention of child labor and exploitation
  • Psychosocial support services
  • Referral services for abused children
Program 02

Education

Promoting access to safe, inclusive, and quality education for children and youth through enrollment support and non-formal programs.

  • Distribution of educational materials
  • School enrollment and retention support
  • Non-formal education programs
  • Literacy and numeracy programs
  • Teacher and community education support
Program 03

Peacebuilding & Social Cohesion

Supporting peaceful coexistence and strengthening relationships among diverse communities affected by conflict and displacement.

  • Community dialogue sessions
  • Conflict resolution training
  • Youth peace initiatives
  • Inter-community peace forums
  • Social cohesion campaigns
Program 04

Climate Change & Environment

Promoting environmental sustainability and climate resilience among communities vulnerable to climate-related challenges.

  • Climate change awareness programs
  • Tree planting campaigns
  • Environmental conservation activities
  • Waste management awareness
  • Climate adaptation support
Program 05

WASH

Improving access to safe water and sanitation facilities while promoting hygiene practices across communities and schools.

  • Hygiene promotion campaigns
  • Distribution of sanitation materials
  • Support for safe water access
  • Community sanitation awareness
  • School-based hygiene education
Program 06

Cultural Heritage

Promoting cultural preservation and encouraging respect for cultural diversity across Nigerian communities.

  • Cultural awareness programs
  • Preservation of local traditions
  • Cultural education activities
  • Community heritage initiatives
Program 07

Health & Nutrition

Promoting health awareness and supporting vulnerable populations with access to nutrition and essential health services.

  • Health education campaigns
  • Nutrition awareness programs
  • Malaria prevention activities
  • Maternal and child health support
  • Community health outreach
Program 08

GBV Response

Preventing and responding to gender-based violence while promoting gender equality and protecting vulnerable individuals.

  • GBV awareness campaigns
  • Survivor support and referral services
  • Gender equality education
  • Community sensitization programs
  • Advocacy against harmful practices
Program 09

Food Security & Livelihoods

Strengthening food security and supporting livelihood opportunities for vulnerable households across intervention communities.

  • Food distribution programs
  • Agricultural support initiatives
  • Vocational training
  • Income-generating activities
  • Livelihood support programs
