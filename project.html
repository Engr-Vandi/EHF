Our Work

Featured Project Areas

Each project area is designed around community needs, local partnerships, and measurable outcomes for people affected by crisis and vulnerability.

Protection

Child Protection & Safeguarding

Community-based prevention, awareness, and referral support that promotes safety and dignity for children.

Education

Inclusive Learning Support

Enrollment support, learning pathways, and school-community engagement for children and young people.

WASH

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene

Hygiene promotion and community WASH activities that reduce health risks and strengthen resilience.

Livelihoods

Food Security & Livelihoods

Support that helps households meet immediate food needs while rebuilding sustainable income pathways.

Peace

Peacebuilding & Social Cohesion

Dialogue, trust-building, and local collaboration that promote peaceful and inclusive communities.

Climate

Climate & Environmental Resilience

Community actions that strengthen environmental awareness, preparedness, and climate-smart practices.

Project Delivery Approach

EHF keeps project planning practical, accountable, and grounded in the realities of the communities it serves.

Assess

02

03
