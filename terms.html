The Fine Print

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: July 2026

Orders & payment

  • Bespoke and custom gowns require a 50% deposit before production begins. The balance is due before delivery or collection.
  • We accept bank transfer (Nigeria), international wire transfer, and mobile money. All payment terms are confirmed in writing before work begins.
  • Quoted prices are confirmed at the time of order. Significant design changes requested after production begins may incur additional charges, which we will always discuss with you first.

Timelines

We recommend ordering bespoke and custom gowns at least 4–6 months before your wedding date to allow time for design, crafting, fittings, and final alterations. Rush orders may be possible depending on our schedule — contact us to discuss your timeline.

Fittings & alterations

One round of alterations is included at no extra cost during the final fitting stage. Additional alteration rounds, or alterations arising from significant weight or measurement changes after production has begun, may be charged separately.

Refunds

Because every bespoke and custom gown is made to order for one bride alone, deposits and payments are non-refundable once production has begun. If we are unable to fulfil an order for reasons within our control, we will refund payments received for work not yet carried out.

Rentals

  • Rental gowns are reserved with a deposit and are typically available for 3–5 days.
  • A refundable damage deposit is required and is returned after the gown is checked in clean and undamaged.
  • Gowns must be returned on the agreed date. Late returns, damage beyond normal wear, or loss will be charged against the damage deposit, and any excess invoiced.
  • Minor alterations for fit can be arranged; permanent modifications to rental gowns are not permitted.

Our craftsmanship promise

Every gown is handmade. Small variations in beadwork, lace placement, and finish are part of the nature of couture and are not defects. If something is genuinely wrong with your gown, tell us at the fitting or upon delivery and we will make it right.

Contact

Questions about these terms? Reach us at Info@giabridals.com or on WhatsApp.
