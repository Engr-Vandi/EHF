About EHF

Who We Are

A registered nonprofit in Nigeria dedicated to vulnerable communities — delivering humanitarian aid, development, and advocacy where it's needed most.

Our Story

Built on Purpose,
Driven by Humanity

Emmanuel Humanitarian Foundation (EHF) was founded to address the persistent humanitarian gaps affecting vulnerable populations across Nigeria, particularly in the North-East and North-Central regions.

The idea emerged from the recognition of urgent needs in communities facing displacement, poverty, gender inequality, food insecurity, and limited access to education and healthcare. Founders and humanitarian volunteers saw the need for an organization that would provide both emergency relief and long-term development support.

Since its establishment, EHF has grown from grassroots awareness campaigns and relief distributions to a multi-thematic organization implementing structured humanitarian and development programs across six states. The foundation continues to expand its partnerships, institutional capacity, and operational reach.

Vision & Mission

The guiding principles behind everything EHF does across Nigeria.

Our Vision

To create a world where people affected by poverty, crisis, and displacement live with dignity, enjoy basic rights, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

To alleviate human suffering, promote sustainable development, and restore hope for all, regardless of background, ethnicity, gender, or religion.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision and action EHF takes in the field.

Humanity

People first — always. Every intervention is centered on human dignity.

Accountability

Answerable to the communities we serve and the donors who trust us.

Transparency

Open, honest reporting on activities, finances, and impact.

Inclusion

No one is left behind — programs serve all regardless of background.

Integrity
