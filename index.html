Who We Are

Built on Purpose,
Driven by Humanity

EHF is a registered nonprofit in Nigeria dedicated to vulnerable and crisis-affected populations — delivering aid, development programs, and human rights advocacy across the country.

Our Vision
A world where people affected by poverty, crisis, and displacement live with dignity and contribute meaningfully to society.
Our Mission
To alleviate human suffering, promote sustainable development, and restore hope for all, regardless of background.
